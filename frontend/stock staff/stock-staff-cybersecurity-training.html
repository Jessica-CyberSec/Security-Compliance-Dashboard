📦 Stock Staff Cybersecurity Training

📌 Objective

Ensure safe and responsible use of warehouse and stockroom digital tools to protect company assets and inventory systems.

🛠️ What You’ll Learn

This training focuses on how stock staff can securely use devices like handheld scanners, tablets, and desktop systems while managing inventory. It provides best practices to prevent unauthorized access and digital threats.

🧠 Situations to Recognize

  • Unattended Device: A tablet left logged in and unattended in the stockroom.
  • Unauthorized App: A staff member installs a non-approved app that collects device data.
  • Public Wi-Fi Access: Using an open network on a scanner while outside the warehouse.

⚠️ Key Cybersecurity Risks

  • Unattended or unlocked shared inventory devices
  • Insecure Wi-Fi usage
  • Installation of unauthorized software
  • Not logging out after use

✅ Best Practices for Stock Staff

  • Always log out and lock devices when unattended
  • Only connect to secure, authorized networks
  • Use approved apps and tools only
  • Report any suspicious device activity to management

📋 Example Scenario

Staff: “I left the scanner unlocked for a quick break.”
You: “Always lock it before stepping away. It prevents misuse or inventory tampering.”

🎯 Final Reminder

Keeping your inventory tools secure is critical. Every device is a gateway to company systems—protect them with care.
