Management Training Descriptions

Data Protection & Compliance: Understand major data protection regulations (e.g., GDPR, PIPEDA) and how to apply them in your business. Learn how to classify data, secure storage methods, and legal timelines for breach notifications. This module empowers managers to enforce data privacy and uphold ethical standards.

Cybersecurity Risk Management: Identify and manage cybersecurity threats within the organization. This training covers insider threats, supply chain risks, cyber insurance, and disaster recovery planning. Managers will learn to build risk registers and develop mitigation strategies to ensure business continuity.
